Give Food

API

We maintain the largest public database of UK food banks, and the items they are requesting to have donated.

This service allows to to get access to this data over HTTPS in JSON, GeoJSON, XML or YAML.

Our API and data has been used by governments, councils, supermarkets, hundreds of national & local news websites, apps & the Trussell Trust.

Rules

  • Do good with the data.
  • Try not to make HTTP requests to us more than a few times a second.
  • If you display a food bank's details then also, if possible, link to them.
  • Lists of items requested should be regarded as a hint rather than perscriptive definition of what's required. If you display what a food bank needs donating, if possible, link to their shopping list URL. There is often nuance to their request.
  • Don't modify lists of items needed.
  • Credit us with a link to our homepage if you republish.
  • Cache, but don't cache for too long. All this is updated at least every few hours.

Assumptions

  • We define a food bank as the organisation.
  • A food bank has one or more locations.

Contact

Please contact us at mail@givefood.org.uk if you're going to launch something using our data. We'll be more than happy to help with any technical questions & optimisations, PR and to ensure that we're all helping food banks as effectively as possible.
