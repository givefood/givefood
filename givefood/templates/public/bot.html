GiveFoodBot

GiveFoodBot is a web crawler from the UK charity Give Food that extracts data from food bank websites including:

  • Addresses
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Items needed and have in excess
  • Charity registration data
  • News articles, including RSS/Atom feeds

The bot identifies itself with this user agent:

{{ BOT_USER_AGENT }}

It should never make more than a few unauthenticated requests per hour to an individual website. If you have a problem with how our bot behaves please do not hesitate to contact us on mail@givefood.org.uk.
