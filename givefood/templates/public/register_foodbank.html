Please use this form to submit the details of a food bank to be included in our database.

This will allow users to see what you are requesting to have donated in our tool, as well as places our data has is used - including hundreds of regional and national newspaper websites, various apps & Trussell Trust.

The UK-based food bank must meet at least two of the following criteria:

  • Be a charity registered with Charity Commission for England and Wales, Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator, or Charity Commission for Northern Ireland. We also consider charities registered in Isle Of Man, Jersey, and Guernsey.
  • Show that food recipients are referred to them by agencies such as a local authority, schools, Citizens Advice Bureau, Jobcentre, GPs, probabtion service or the police.
  • Have a published strategy for supporting the food recipient so as they do not become reliant on the foodbank in the long term.
  • Be a member of Trussell Trust or IFAN.

In order to use our service fully and for us to automatically discover what donations the food bank is requesting we will need a website address of your shopping list.
