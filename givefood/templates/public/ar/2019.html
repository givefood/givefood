2019 Annual Report

We're a UK organisation that uses technology to deliver the food needed to foodbanks. Every day we monitor foodbank's needs thousand of times - you can see how we do this on our homepage.

In the meantime, here's a breakdown of what we've done in 2019.

In 2019 Give Food delivered 60,479kg of food and supplies consisting of 123,344 items. This includes...

5.65

Also, during the Christmas season we delivered 1980 mince pies!

We've checked foodbank websites and social media for food needs about 2,111,160 times, downloading and parsing over 2.8 TB of data.

All this was delivered to 154 food banks across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.
